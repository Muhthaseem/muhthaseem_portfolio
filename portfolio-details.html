MediVision

A mobile app for early detection of skin diseases using image processing and machine learning.

The app allows users to upload images of skin conditions, identifies potential diseases, and provides treatment suggestions. If the disease cannot be identified, users are encouraged to seek medical consultation.

The use of AI in healthcare is not a novel concept. Machine learning models have been widely employed in radiology, pathology, and other medical disciplines to identify abnormalities with high accuracy. In dermatology, AI has shown promise in diagnosing conditions like psoriasis, dermatitis, and various types of skin cancer. MediVision will build on this foundation by developing a system tailored to the needs of everyday users, enabling them to access reliable diagnostic tools from the convenience of their smartphones.

By sourcing training data from verified internet pages and trusted medical resources, the project ensures that the AI model is both robust and inclusive. This approach not only enhances the accuracy of predictions but also addresses potential biases in existing datasets. MediVision aims to be a transformative solution that combines the power of AI with practical usability, empowering users to take charge of their skin health.
